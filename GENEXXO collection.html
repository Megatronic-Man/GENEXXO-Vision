Finite Categories represent well-defined fields like Beauty, Fashion, and Gift, where comprehensive coverage means securing every significant element. Here, GENEXXO has acquired virtually every meaningful term within these sectors, creating extensive market representation. The value lies in owning not just individual terms, but the breadth of the semantic space that defines these categories.

The 14 Golden Rules

The collection's strength emerges not just from its size, but from the principles that guided its assembly. Domain selection followed 14 Golden Rules that ensure maximum strategic value:

  1. Exclusive .com focus – the internet's most valuable real estate
  2. Single-word purity – over 95% single words with XX suffix
  3. English language primacy – ensuring global appeal
  4. Trademark safety – avoiding protected terms and brand names
  5. Generic term focus – maximizing broad applicability
  6. Complete word integrity – widely-recognized terminology only
  7. Clean format – no hyphens or numbers
  8. Regional spelling coverage – territory-specific variations
  9. Categorical clarity – every domain serves a defined purpose
  10. B2B and B2C balance – comprehensive market coverage
  11. Future-proofing – foundational language selection ensuring timeless commercial relevance while securing emerging trends
  12. Strategic plurals & variations – addressing different user behaviors and search patterns
  13. Global market consideration – cultural and regional awareness
  14. Functional categorization – clear purpose within the ecosystem
